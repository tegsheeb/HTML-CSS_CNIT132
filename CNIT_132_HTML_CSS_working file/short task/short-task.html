Our History

Unlock Podcast is one of the pioneering Mongolian podcasts, in which hosts read bestselling nonfiction books in English and summarize, critique & discuss their takeaways in the Mongolian language, dedicated for the Mongolian-speaking audience. The episodes are usually in an one-hour format and delivered to the listeners via various podcast apps on smart phones, computers and other smart gadgets, without any charge.

The first eposide was aired on June 9, 2017 with "Elon Musk, Tesle , SpaceX and the Quest for a Fantastic Future" by Ashlee Vance. Since then to date, 55 episodes featuring 52 bestselling nonfiction books have been delivered to the Mongolian audience living around the world.
